# Bibliography & References

Copies of the academic papers and reference charts used to build DrumScript are available below.

## Academic Papers
* [**Hybrid Transformers for Music Source Separation**](references/HYBRID_TRANSFORMERS.pdf)  
    *The foundational paper for the Demucs engine.*
* [**Real-Time Automatic Drum Transcription**](references/Published_Real-Time_Automatic_Drum_Transcription.pdf)  
    *Research on dynamic few-shot learning for drums.*
* [**Improving Perceptual Quality of Drum Transcription**](references/Improving_Perceptual_Quality.pdf)  
    *Google Magenta's research on the Expanded Groove MIDI Dataset.*

## Standards & Charts
* [**General MIDI Percussion Key Map**](references/General_MIDI_Standard_Percussion_Set_Key_Map.pdf)  
    *The standard mapping of notes to drum sounds (e.g., 36 = Kick).*
* [**Master Drum Key**](references/Master-Drum-Key_R2.pdf)  
    *A comprehensive guide to standard notation symbols.*
